About Us

Our roots began in Hyderabad where our renowned chefs Rahul Malhotra & Jigmet Wangchuk studied the craft of authentic South Indian cuisine since 1975. Both are Michelin-Star rated chefs and had a dream of opening a restaurant which catered the food they both grew up eating. SOUTHY will be the ultimate delight for your tastebuds and an experience you will never forget.

Menu

Desserts

Payasam

Payasam is the one dessert that is savoured in the South regardless of age and culture - a sweetened pudding cooked with coconut milk, sugar and dairy milk that will entice the gastronomical senses.


Mysore Pak

Mysore Pak does wonders to a sweet lovers palate. Made from generous amounts of ghee, sugar, gram flour and fragrant cardamom, it is hard to resist another piece of it.


Rava Kesari with Milk

South India's Rava Kesari is an absolute treat with its creamy and roasty after taste. Made from saffron, mangoes, pineapples, cahsews and raisins


Maladu

Maladu is a protein rich treat. It is made from roasted gram dal or chutney dal (pottukadalai). Made in mere 15 minutes using less than six ingredients.Not only are they 'melt in the mouth' good but are also nutritious.


Unni Appam

Similar to the soft and sugary gulab jamuns, it is a popular delicacy in Kerala made from rice, jaggery, banana, roasted pieces of coconut, roasted ground sesame seeds, ghee and cardamom powder.


Kozhukkattai

In Tamil Nadu this popular treat is associated with Lord Ganesha and is prepared as an offering on the religious occasion of Vinayaka Chathurthi.This sweet dumpling is made from rice flour, with a filling of grated coconut and jaggery.

Menu

Drinks

Jigarthanda

One of the best tasting summer drink which has its origins in Madurai.The main ingredients of jigarthanda recipe are reduced full fat milk, reduced milk ice cream and two natural coolants i.e, badam pisin (edible almond gum) and nannari (sarsaparilla roots) syrup.


Mongo Iced Tea

Mango iced tea is a delicious, tropical summer beverage that combines two ingredients, tea and ripe mango puree.. A winning combination with a feel good factor.


Falooda

a milk based dessert, has rose syrup, falooda sev or vermicelli made from arrowroot or corn flour, milk, falooda seeds (basil seeds) saffron, pista, mango, strawberry, chocolate and ice cream going into it.


Fruit Lassi

Yoghurt mixed with fresh fruits like apple, banana, mango, grapes and musk melon. Along with a medley of fruity flavors, fruit lassi is loaded with vitamins and antioxidants and has a natural sweetness.
