About Us

Our roots began in Hyderabad where our renowned chefs Rahul Malhotra & Jigmet Wangchuk studied the craft of authentic South Indian cuisine since 1975. Both are Michelin-Star rated chefs and had a dream of opening a restaurant which catered the food they both grew up eating. SOUTHY will be the ultimate delight for your tastebuds and an experience you will never forget.
